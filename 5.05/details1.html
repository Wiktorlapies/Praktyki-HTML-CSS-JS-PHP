Raspberry Pi IoT Projects

Autor: John C. Shovic

Cena: 35.50zł

ISBN: 978-1-484213-78-0

Ilość: 13

Strony: 256

Opis: This book is designed for entry-through-intermediate-level device designers who want to build their own Internet of Things (IoT) projects for prototyping and proof-of-concept purposes. Expert makers may also find interesting new approaches. Raspberry Pi IoT Projects contains the tools needed to build a prototype of your design, sense the environment, communicate with the Internet (over the Internet and Machine to Machine communications) and display the results.
